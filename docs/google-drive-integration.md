# Google Drive Integration

Connect Google Drive to RetrieveIt.AI to search your documents, spreadsheets, and files stored in Drive.

## Overview

The Google Drive integration allows you to:
- Import documents from your Google Drive
- Search Google Docs, Sheets, Slides, and uploaded files
- Keep content synced automatically

## Setting Up Google Drive Integration

### Step 1: Connect Your Account

1. Go to your **Workspace** in the Dashboard
2. Find the **Google Drive Integration** section
3. Click **Connect Google Drive**
4. Sign in with your Google account
5. Grant RetrieveIt.AI permission to access your Drive

### Step 2: Initial Sync

After connecting:
1. RetrieveIt.AI will scan your Drive
2. Supported files are imported and indexed
3. Once complete, content is searchable in Chat

## Managing Your Connection

### Manual Sync
Click **Sync Now** to pull in new or updated files. Automatic syncs also happen periodically.

### Disconnect
Click **Disconnect** to remove the Google Drive connection. This deletes all synced documents from the workspace.

## What Gets Indexed

### Google Workspace Files
- **Google Docs** - Documents are converted to text
- **Google Sheets** - Spreadsheet content is extracted
- **Google Slides** - Presentation text is indexed

### Uploaded Files
Files uploaded to Drive in supported formats:
- PDF documents
- Microsoft Office files (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
- Text and Markdown files
- Images with text (OCR may be limited)

## Folder Structure

Your Drive folder structure is preserved, making it easy to identify where documents came from in search results.

## Shared Drives

If you have access to Shared Drives (Team Drives), those files can also be indexed based on your permissions.

## Tips

- Great for searching company wikis and documentation in Google Docs
- Use alongside Gmail integration for complete Google Workspace search
- Combine with other integrations for unified knowledge search
- Works well for teams already using Google Workspace

## Privacy & Security

- RetrieveIt.AI only reads files you have access to
- We don't modify or delete any files in your Drive
- Content is processed and stored securely
- Disconnect anytime to remove all synced data
- Your Drive permissions are respected
