# How to Sign In to RetrieveIt.AI

RetrieveIt.AI uses **magic links** for passwordless authentication - no password to remember or manage.

## How Magic Links Work

1. Enter your email address on the login page
2. Click "Send Magic Link"
3. Check your email for a message from `noreply@retrieveit.ai`
4. Click the link in the email
5. You're signed in!

## Important Notes

- Magic links expire after **15 minutes**
- Each link can only be used **once**
- Links are tied to your browser session

## Troubleshooting

### Magic Link Not Arriving

1. **Wait 2-3 minutes** - emails can be delayed
2. **Check Spam/Junk** folder
3. **Check email filters** you may have created
4. **Verify your email** - make sure you typed it correctly

### Magic Link Expired

If your link expired:
1. Go back to the login page
2. Enter your email again
3. Request a fresh magic link

### Gmail Users

If magic links go to spam in Gmail:
1. Check your Spam folder and mark as "Not spam"
2. Add `noreply@retrieveit.ai` to your contacts

### Google Workspace Organizations

If your company uses Google Workspace, your IT admin may need to allowlist `retrieveit.ai` in the admin console.

## Security Tips

- Don't forward magic link emails - they're tied to your session
- Keep your email account secure - anyone with access can log in
- Log out when using shared computers
